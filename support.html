Frequently Asked Questions

Is OnePath free?

Yes. OnePath is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases.

Where does the data come from?

All immigration data is sourced from official government websites: USCIS.gov, Travel.State.gov, the Department of Labor, and the Federal Register. Data is verified and updated regularly.

How often is the data updated?

We update the Visa Bulletin monthly, processing times quarterly, and policy changes as they happen. The app shows the last update date in the footer of the home screen.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your personal information stays on your device. We don't collect your name, email, visa status, or any personally identifiable information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I change my answers after onboarding?

Yes. Tap the gear icon on the home screen to open Settings. You can change any of your profile answers individually.

Can I use OnePath offline?

Yes. All immigration data is bundled with the app. The only features that require internet are external links to government websites and the legal aid directory.

Is OnePath a substitute for an immigration lawyer?

No. OnePath provides immigration information for educational purposes only. Every immigration case is unique. We strongly recommend consulting a licensed immigration attorney for advice specific to your situation.

How do I reset the app?

Go to Settings (gear icon on home screen), scroll to the bottom, and tap "Reset App and Start Over." This clears all data and lets you redo the onboarding.

I found incorrect information. How do I report it?

Please email us at onepathworld@gmail.com with the specific information that appears incorrect. We take data accuracy very seriously and will investigate and correct any errors promptly.
