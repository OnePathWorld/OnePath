OnePath Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Our Commitment

OnePath is an immigration information app built with your privacy in mind. We believe your immigration data is deeply personal, and we've designed the app to keep it that way.

The short version: Your personal data stays on your device. When you use the Case Status feature, your USCIS receipt number is sent securely to USCIS on your behalf to retrieve your case status. We do not store your receipt number or case results.

What Data Stays on Your Device

All information you enter during onboarding and while using the app is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. This includes:

  • Your immigration purpose and goals
  • Country of citizenship
  • Current visa status
  • Checklist progress
  • Compliance tracking data

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server, except as described in the Case Status Lookup section below.

USCIS Case Status Lookup

OnePath offers an optional feature that allows you to check your USCIS case status by entering your receipt number (e.g. EAC2390123456).

When you use this feature:

  • Your receipt number is sent securely to the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) API on your behalf
  • USCIS returns your case status directly to your device
  • We do not store your receipt number on our servers
  • We do not store your case status results
  • The query is made in real time and discarded immediately after delivery to your device
Important: Your receipt number is sensitive immigration information. OnePath transmits it only to the official USCIS API and retains no record of your query.

This feature is governed by USCIS's own privacy policy, available at uscis.gov/privacy.

Anonymous Analytics

We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app. This includes:

  • Which screens are viewed
  • Which features are used (e.g., tapping a pathway, switching tabs)
  • General device information (OS version, app version)
  • Approximate location (city-level, from IP address)

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your exact immigration status or visa details
  • Your passport or document information
  • Any information that could identify you personally

Analytics data is used solely to understand which features are most useful and to improve the app experience.

No Accounts Required

OnePath does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address. There is no user registration system.

No Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. We do not display advertisements. We do not use your data for marketing purposes.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by going to Settings within the app and tapping "Reset App and Start Over." This permanently removes all locally stored data. You can also uninstall the app, which removes all data from your device.

Children's Privacy

OnePath is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
